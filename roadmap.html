Our Journey

Roadmap

Our roadmap outlines our strategic vision for building a sustainable and thriving Web3 ecosystem, from initial launch to future expansion.

Phase 1

Pre-Launch

Establishment of the core legal framework, initial team formation, and development of the foundational technology and strategic partnerships.

Phase 2

Launch

The official Token Generation Event (TGE), deployment of our core platform, and the initiation of our first community engagement campaigns.

Phase 3

Growth

Scaling the ecosystem through strategic marketing, onboarding new partners and KOLs, and expanding our suite of AI-powered retention tools.

Phase 4

Expansion

Exploring new markets, launching advanced features, and further decentralizing governance to empower our community of stakeholders.
